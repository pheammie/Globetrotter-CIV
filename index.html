About Us

A gaming company dedicated to immersing you in the local culture and life of your chosen destination. A visa to be a part of the community from wherever you are. This is a cultural immersion idea to bring home to you wherever you are.

Our Purpose

Combat Nostalgia

Reduce or ameliorate nostalgia of people far from home by bringing the experience of being back at home to them wherever they are.

Cultural Education

Educate people on culture, norms and way of different people/cultures/countries.

Heritage Connection

Educate 2nd or more generations of migrants born abroad about their ancestry and life in their fatherland/motherland.

Cultural Integration

Reduce information asymmetry and culture shocks experienced by visitors to a new locale.
